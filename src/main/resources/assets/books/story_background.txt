This story is about a lonely person landed on a place that consists of many dangers and lives.

However, air wizard which is a great wizard sealed the door to the obsidian knight, who is a guardian of potential growth, long time ago. The air wizard is also a curse of alone, leading no life giving friendly responses to the main character.

Therefore, no friendly living things could have grouped together, including the person itself.

If the curse is broken, everything could be normal again as like before, but monsters still exist.
